About Pedestrian Detection

In this project, Faster R-CNN with MobileNetV2 as its backbone is used for pedestrian detection. The combination leverages MobileNetV2's lightweight and efficient architecture to enhance Faster R-CNN's object detection capabilities, allowing for real-time and accurate pedestrian identification in resource-constrained environments. This approach balances high performance with computational efficiency, making it suitable for applications like autonomous driving and surveillance.
